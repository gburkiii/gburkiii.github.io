Other Streams
A USC Roski Group Show at the Helen Topping Fine Arts and Architecture Library

ABOUT THE EXHIBITION:
Other Streams is a group exhibition utilizing artwork as research in collaboration with the Architecture + Fine Arts Library.
Navigating the fluidity of creative cycles and the waters of the conscious and unconscious, we aim to resurrect the library as a space of play and searching. Treating the library as a labyrinth, a cave, and an underground repository of knowledge, we map our internal paths to nourish and inform our external derives. Through our tapestry of works we drift through ways of making outside our known processes.
Exhibition on view: Nov. 20 - Dec. 10, 2024
